Rover robots are versatile robotic vehicles designed for traversing diverse terrain, conducting exploration, and performing various tasks autonomously or under remote control. These robots often feature multiple wheels or tracks for mobility and are equipped with sensors, cameras, and other instruments for navigation, mapping, and data collection. Rover robots are utilized in a wide range of applications, including planetary exploration, terrestrial research, mining, agriculture, environmental monitoring, and search and rescue operations.
